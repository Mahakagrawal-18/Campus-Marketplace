<h1>Campus Marketplace</h1>
<h3>Rebuilding the Broken College Marketplace</h3>
<p>Campus Marketplace is a college-exclusive buying and selling platform designed to help students securely exchange items such as electronics, books, cycles, hostel essentials, and academic resources within their campus community.
The platform replaces fragmented systems like WhatsApp groups and informal communication channels with a centralized, trusted digital marketplace.</P>
<h3>Problem Statement</h3>
<P>Resource exchange in colleges currently happens through unorganized platforms such as personal contacts or messaging groups, leading to:
Lack of trust between buyers and sellers ,No structured listing system ,Risk of fraud or fake deals ,Difficulty in discovering required items ,No transaction tracking.Students adapt to inefficient systems instead of improving them.</P>
<h3>Our Solution</h3>
<p>Campus Marketplace introduces a verified campus ecosystem where students can:
  <ul>
    <li>Buy and sell items safely</li>
    <li>Upload product listings</li>
    <li>Browse categorized items</li>
    <li>Perform secure peer-to-peer transactions</li>
    <li>Use escrow-based deal confirmation</li>
    <li>Maintain trust through verified users</li>
    
  </ul>
Only college members can participate, ensuring reliability.</p>
